FAQs
Lenders
How much money can I make?
There is no cap on how much money you can make. Remember we charge 5% of the total rental cost as a commission though.
Is it safe to rent my items?
It is absolutely safe to rent your items as each member on the website has to pass the two stage verification process. If you are still not sure, you can always purchase insurance!
How do I put my product to rent?
Just click on the Lend button and it will open up a form for you to fill. Enter all the details required and hit submit button. That will put your item out for rent. Yes...its that simple!
Renters
How do I rent items?
You just have to search for the item you want in the search bar, along with the location and hit search. Select the best suited item for you from the list and then rent it! You will have to pay for the item though...but we ensure it will be a lot cheaper than renting it from any other places!
What is the difference between Instant Rent and Message buttons?
Instant rent will help you to instantly rent an item. No approval needed from the lender. Message lender will help you to talk to the lender to hash out some questions before renting the item. Please be aware that the system will ask for your credit card information in both cases when you checkout. Your credit card will be charged instantly when you choose instant rent but will not charge you until the lender approves when you choose message option.
What is the Negotiate button?
Negotiate is a cool feature that will allow you to haggle the price of the item with the lender. Be aware when using this feature, you don't want to make the lender angry by suggesting very low prices. To help you with that, we will recommend you an ideal price that you can negotiate!
